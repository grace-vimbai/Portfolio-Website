My Story

I have always been two things, an artist and a problem solver. As an International Studies Student, my intellectual curiosity led me to hone my problem solving skills by applying them to world issues. Through various international opportunities I was able to develop my abilities in research and program management, contributing to teams dedicated to finding innovative solutions to global problems. Although the work was interesting, I started to notice a deep dissatisfaction with the lack of creative/artistic outlets in my professional life. Some roles would lean on my self taught skills in graphic design, photography and digital illustration every now and then, but design was never central to the problem solving processes I was a part of.
